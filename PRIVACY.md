# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

NoteCast is built to be private by design. This document explains what happens
to your data — the short version is: **nothing leaves your machine.**

## This website

The NoteCast marketing website collects **no data at all**. There are no
analytics, no tracking scripts, no cookies, no ad networks, and no third-party
requests. Fonts are self-hosted, so simply visiting the site does not report
anything to us or anyone else.

## The NoteCast extension

- **Audio, transcripts, and notes stay on your device.** Tab audio is captured,
  transcribed (with Whisper), and summarized (with your local Ollama model)
  entirely on your own computer.
- **No cloud, no server, no account.** There is no NoteCast backend to send your
  data to, and you never create an account or provide an API key.
- **No telemetry.** The extension does not phone home or send usage statistics.
- **Local storage only.** Session history and notes are stored locally in your
  browser and can be exported (Markdown or plain text) or deleted by you at any
  time.
- **One-time model download.** The first capture downloads the Whisper model
  weights once; afterward they are cached and the extension works offline.
